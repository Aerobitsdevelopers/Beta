about

Established in the year 2005, we Raamz Interiors and Architecture kicked off as a small interior design firm based at Kochi. Later on, by stepping into construction services and expanding our services throughout South India, we now stands out as one of the major and renowned Architecture firms in Kerala.

We take up all kinds of Residential works and Commercial projects with our services provided all over Kerala covering urban and rural areas. We Raamz Interiors & Architecture, have the tradition of delivering more than what is expected from us, in service, commitment and punctuality.

Design
Philosophy

Authenticity is about knowing and embracing your personal attributes and how they contribute to the designs that you love. The essence of the design is its authenticity. Others can sense the warmth when its convenient for you and your community.

Therefore, here at Raamz Interiors and Architecture, we interpret our design ethos as creating an aesthetic of style and sophistication, but also rooted in the firm belief that our role is to create spaces symbolicof our clients, and as a team we work towards the aim of creative, personalized solutions that represent the best of our clients.
