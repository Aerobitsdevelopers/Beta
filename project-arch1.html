Project: Vagamon, Kerala

The inspiration arose from the traditional architecture of different historical eras from which the design guidelines for contemporary tropical architecture have emerged. These architectures had to consider the creation of shade, cross ventilation, the abundance of rain, the sun and the heat, the gloom of the interior spaces, the sky and its low clouds as a surrounding landscape, steeply sloping roofs, and the lush vegetation.The resort was designed to be a natural defense against the harsh elements of the tropics and subtropics, while maximizing the advantage of living in a warm and sunny environment.
